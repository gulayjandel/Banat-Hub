Our Story

Bohol's #1 Fast Food Hub

Taste of Tradition

Starting from the heart of Bohol, Banat Hub was founded on a simple mission: to serve authentic, high-quality Filipino flavors to every hungry soul. What began as a local favorite has grown into a premier food destination, known for our signature boodle-style presentation and farm-to-table freshness.

Our Mission

To provide affordable, delicious, and culturally rich dining experiences that bring families together, one banana leaf at a time.

Our Vision

To be the leading representative of Philippine fast-food excellence, expanding our reach while maintaining the authentic taste of home.
